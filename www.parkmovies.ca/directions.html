The Bandshell area of Waterloo Park is in the WEST end of the park.

View Map

or

Use the "get directions" on Google Maps.

You have several options for parking here:

  1. Westmount Rd. - Enter the park gate between David Bauer Drive & University Ave.
  2. Seagram Dr. - Enter from Seagram Dr. (next to the railway crossing)
  3. David Bauer Dr. - There is a parking lot across the street from the Waterloo Recreation Complex. Walk past the baseball diamonds, and past the Wesmount Rd. parking lot.
  4. Westmount Plaza.
  5. Waterloo Recreation Complex
  6. Park at home and walk.
  7. Take the bus.
